CRAWDAD Dataset Contribution Form: Start Here!

Thank you for offering to contribute your data to CRAWDAD! If we will be only mirroring data that is already hosted elsewhere, only your permission is needed. However, if your data will be only hosted at CRAWDAD, you will need to sign this agreement form. Please ensure that you are able to sign the agreement before proceeding with submission of the description of your data. Please mail your signed agreement to crawdad-team@cs.dartmouth.edu.
